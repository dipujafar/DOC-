Frequntly Asked Question

What are your office hours?

Our office hours vary by location, but typically we are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Some locations may offer extended hours or weekend appointments. Please contact your nearest clinic for specific hours.

How can I schedule an appointment?

To schedule a doctor's appointment online, visit the healthcare provider's website and navigate to the "Appointments" section. Choose the type of appointment you need and select a doctor or specialist. Pick a suitable date and time, provide necessary personal and insurance information, and confirm the appointment.

Do you accept insurance?

To inquire about insurance acceptance, visit the healthcare provider's website and check the "Insurance" or "Billing" section. Alternatively, contact their office directly via phone or email to confirm if your insurance plan is accepted before scheduling an appointment.

What should I bring to my appointment?

For your appointment, bring a photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications, medical history, and any relevant medical records. Additionally, bring a form of payment for any co-pays, and a list of questions or concerns you want to discuss with the doctor.

Do you offer telemedicine appointments?

To find out if telemedicine appointments are offered, check the healthcare provider's website under the "Telemedicine" or "Virtual Visits" section. You can also call or email their office for details on availability, how to schedule, and the technology required for virtual appointments.
